About Tristar Martial Arts

I train at Tristar Martial Arts Academy, in Mount Airy, Maryland.

While the martial art itself doesn't have too many belt ranks, Tristar's belt rank progression gives you a sense of accomplishment while still keeping you motivated to keep advancing.

Here is how Tristar breaks down the classes and belt ranks included in each class.

Class Belt Ranks
Basic White, Yellow
Intermediate Orange, Green, Purple, Blue
Advanced Red, Brown, Brown with 1 stripe, Brown with 2 stripes
Black Belt Prep Advanced Brown, Advanced Brown with 1 stripe, Advanced Brown with 2 Stripes, Advanced Brown with 3 Stripes
