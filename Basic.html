Basic (White and Yellow)

In July 2019, I moved up from the younger classes into the Basic class.

This is the class that any person aged 7 or older begins in.

Earning a promotion in this class is much more difficult, and is not guaranteed.

In order to earn a promotion in this class, you need to know techniques and terminology. You are tested on these things twice before you earn a stripe.

If you are unable to meet the expectations, you don't get a stripe. If you fail to acquire the three stripes necessary, you fail to promote.

I promoted out of the basic class in December 2019.
