Ninja Kid

After spending at least 6 months in the Little Ninja program, kids under 7 promote to the "Ninja Kid" program.

I finished the Little Ninja program and promoted into the Ninja Kid program in January 2019.

The Ninja Kid program builds on those fundamentals you learn in Little Ninjas.

Things you learn in Ninja Kids that you do not learn in Little Ninjas are:

  • Kicks (Side Kick)
  • Punches (Straight, Horizontal)
  • How to get away from strangers who have grabbed you.
