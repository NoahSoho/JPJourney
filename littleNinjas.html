Little Ninja

At Tristar, 4 to 6 year olds begin their martial arts training as a "Little Ninja".

As a Little Ninja, you learn some basic things that serve as the foundation for your learning, like:

  • Blocks (Up, down, side)
  • Kicks (front snap, heel stomp)
  • Punches (hammer fist, palm strike)

Also in Little Ninjas, you learn what bullies and strangers are, and how to defend and get away from them.
